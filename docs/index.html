Services

Company formation, accounting, certified translations, bank account opening, licensing, legal support, and full administrative assistance — fast, reliable, and fully tailored to your needs

  • Company Formation

    Company Formation (s.r.o.) — Complete Service Package We prepare all documents required for establishing a company, guide you through every step, and handle all legal procedures. Fast, transparent and error-free incorporation.

  • Document Preparation

    Complete Document Preparation for Company Registration We collect all necessary documentation, prepare applications, ensure compliance with legal requirements, and manage submissions to the official registry.

  • Business Licenses and Permits

    Obtaining Business Licenses and Permits We arrange trade licenses, special permits, and all documentation required for compliant business activity in the Czech Republic. Fast and professional assistance.

  • Representation in Government Offices

    Representation in Public Institutions We handle administrative procedures at the trade office, financial office, immigration authorities, social office, and other institutions. No need for you to attend in person.

  • Certified Translations

    Certified and Court-Approved Translations Official translations for government offices, banks, courts, and notaries. All documents are translated and certified by authorized sworn translators.

  • Bank Account Opening

    Opening Personal and Corporate Bank Accounts We prepare the required documents, assist during the bank visit, and help you choose the most suitable banking conditions. Cooperation with major Czech banks.

  • Legal Support for Real Estate

    Legal Support for Real Estate Transactions We review properties, prepare agreements, coordinate negotiations, and ensure safe and legally sound property acquisition. Full assistance throughout the transaction.

  • Accounting Services

    Full Accounting Support We provide bookkeeping, prepare financial statements, handle VAT submissions, control deadlines, and help optimize your tax obligations. Reliable and confidential accounting for businesses and freelancers.
